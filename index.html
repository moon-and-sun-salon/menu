WELCOME

Moon and Sun beauty salon, an independently owned and operated hair salon, is located in beautiful Yangon city, Myanmar. We are proud to indulge guests from anywhere. We are a full-service Hair Salon that caters to each client's unique style and personality. Escape to a world of comfort, relaxation, and rejuvention. Treat yourself to a wonderful hair style, color/highlighting, or manicure/pedicure. We understand the mind-body-spirit connection in hair care.

“How old you are is your business, but how young you look is our business." We can create the look that you want or rebuild hair that has been damaged by stress and environmental factors.

We provide services, hair cutting, hair treatment, perming, rebonding, and hair coloring, manicure, and pedicure. We only use quality products and supplies. I would like to introduce what kind of quality products we use on our customers.

Olaplex

No.1 is meant to help mitigate damage during the color, lightening and relaxing process, however it cannot stop all of it. If it did, you would not get a visible result. No.2 is not an activator, neutralizer, sealer or conditioner. No.2 contains the same active ingredient as No.1. No.2 finds more single sulfur hydrogen bonds and cross links them back together without color, lightener or relaxers working against it.
